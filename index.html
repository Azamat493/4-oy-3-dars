How can I get into Meta RL?

1️⃣ You can start by building a strong foundation in reinforcement learning and machine learning — take courses on deep RL, replicate open-source projects, and contribute to AI research communities. Meta looks for people who can demonstrate real understanding and innovation, not just theory.

2️⃣ Apply through Meta Careers with a solid portfolio — focus on projects using PyTorch (Meta’s own framework), publish papers if possible, and show practical RL applications like game agents or robotics. Networking with current Meta researchers also helps a lot.

3️⃣ Getting into Meta RL isn’t about luck — it’s about visibility. Share your RL experiments on GitHub, write posts about your findings, and engage in AI conferences or online discussions. Meta scouts for people who are both skilled and active in the community.

When is the NFT launch?

1️⃣ Start by learning the basics of reinforcement learning (RL) — concepts like rewards, policies, and Q-learning. Then explore Meta’s open research papers on RL and try to reproduce some experiments using PyTorch.

2️⃣ Network with Meta engineers on LinkedIn and contribute to open-source RL frameworks. Showing initiative and technical skill can help you get noticed when internship or research opportunities open.

3️⃣ Focus on building a strong portfolio. Create small RL projects, like game-playing agents or robotics simulations, and document them on GitHub. Meta looks for candidates who can show both theory and implementation skill.

Where can I get a WAVE NFT?

1️⃣ You can mint or buy WAVE NFTs directly from their official marketplace when drops are live — just make sure you connect your wallet (like MetaMask) and verify the contract address to avoid scams.

2️⃣ Check secondary NFT platforms such as OpenSea or Blur — search for “WAVE NFT” collections and look for verified creators to ensure authenticity before purchasing.

3️⃣ Sometimes WAVE NFTs are available only through exclusive community events or collaborations. Join their Discord or follow the official Twitter/X account to catch upcoming airdrops and whitelist opportunities early.
